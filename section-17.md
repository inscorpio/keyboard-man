## macro

- `q{register}`: start
- `q`: end
- `:reg {register}`: view register
- `@{register}`: play macro
- `@@`: play macro which the last play
- `{count}@{register}`: play macro repeatedly

- `q{upper case register}`: append

- `:"{register}p`: change
- `:put {register}`: change
